How

How should you start

First of all you need a skatboard to get started. You want to be comfortable on you board a lot of people will want to jump straight to doing tricks but it is important to have a strong foundation or else you'll end up getting hurt.

Skill level Reccomended tricks
Beginner Riding, Pushing, Ollie, Boneless
Novice Shoveit, Pop Shoveit, BS/FS 180
Intemediate Kickflip, Heelflip, fakie Bigspin, Varial Kickflip
Advanced Tre Flip, Varial Heelflip, Hardflip, BS/FS flips

Why

Why you should start skating

  • Good way to exercise and be active
  • Meet a lot of people help you progress in the sport
  • Can reduce stress, having a sense of freedom and accomplishment
  • Can boost confidence and perserverance
  • You can skate anywhere parks, street, backyard ect.
